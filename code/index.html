What is Kitten Transfer?

Kitten Transfer is a service for kitten owners who enjoy transferring their kitten quickly and at low cost. Kittens of all sizes and shapes can be transferred, just make sure you have a reliable internet connection!

Why would you want to transfer a kitten?

Oh a number of reasons! For example, have you ever had a cat on an airplane? Oh those poor things - hungry and afraid! Why put your darling through that.

Are there any side-effects?

We are happy to say that there are no known side-effects to our Kitten Transfers. Years of R&D has really paid off!
