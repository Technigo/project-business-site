FAQ

Food and drinks? ▼

We offer a range of food and beverage stalls inside the festival area as well as in the camping area. There are Vegan alternatives at some of the stalls. Water is free at the festival area and the camping area.

Where can I park? ▼

There are thousands parking lots next to the festival area, and also in the camping are. To be sure that you have a parking lot you need to book one which costs 100 SEK a day.

Running order ▼

We will soon announce which day the bands/artists will play. Playscheme will be avaible one month before the festival.

Is there any age limit? ▼

Yes, you must be at least 18 years old.

What genres of metal? ▼

We want to explore every genre of metal. Please send us an email if you have any suggestions/whishes.
