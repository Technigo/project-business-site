Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I learn to dance?

By stepping onto the dance floor, you are improving many areas of your life. It will not only boost your confidence, mood and social skills, but your body tone and posture, too. Learning to dance is more than an investment in technique; it is an investment in a fun and healthier lifestyle.

Can parents be present during the class?

Dance class is a new world for your child to discover, and all students must be comfortable in their environment for them to flourish. For this reason, family members are not invited into the classroom.

Can I try a class out first to see if I like it?

We offer “Open Class” weeks, which is the best time to try out a new style.

Do I need to buy special dance shoes/uniform for my child?

Yes, a special uniform is required for most of the classes. Dance class involves physical activity and we must ensure comfort and safety of the child.
