FAQ

Who runs the Environment Show?

At the moment just me, Haru Ahn.

Why are you doing this?

All the Earth’s systems are under stress from humans right now. (It’s not just climate change we need to worry about.) The solutions are at hand. My goal is to help take sustainability from the fringes to the mainstream. We owe it to the next generation.

Can I work with you?

Possibly yes. We are looking for talented, committed folk to work with us. To find out how you can do it, please check out our ‘Contribute’ page.

How can I meet like-minded green people?

We recommend Green Drinks for live meetups in cities around the world. There are all sorts of green groups online as well. You’re welcome to join The Environment Show social group on Facebook. If you’re working in the communications industry, you can join a professional community of green-thinking media people online at Green Communicators. It’s really worthwhile to hook up with your tribe. There are smart, environmentally minded people in many corners of society. You are not alone!

How can I contact you?

Still haven’t found what you’re looking for? You can get in touch with me via the Contact page. Whilst I do my best to answer every email, sometimes I don’t get to all of them – as I’m juggling a number of projects. Sometimes the answer is already in the FAQs and info here on the website. (So best to have a look first before sending a message.) All that said, I’ll do my best to help if I can! Cheers, Haru
