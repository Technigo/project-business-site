Frequently Asked Questions

What is Friendly Fashion?

Friendly Fashion is a clothing rental service.

Why rent?

Europe throws away 2,080,544 tonnes of unused clothes every year. Renting is a great way to decrease your climate imprint.

How does it work?

Step 1. Choose items from our closet. Step 2. Our bike Messenger will deliver your items to your door. We also pickup at your door. 3. Switch items whenever you please.

How much does it cost?

Our monthly fee is 600kr. Cancel or pause your subscription whenever you want.
