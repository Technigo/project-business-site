Solar FAQ

How big is a solar module?

One solar module is usually 1,7 - 2m high and 1m wide, the weight is usually 20-25kg.

How much of the sun can be used?

The most efficient solarmodules can usually harness 20% of the energy from the sun, however there is also some losses in the system to convert the electricity.

How much of the electricity in Sweden comes from solar?

According to data 0,4% of the electricity in 2019 was generated by solar (source).

How much solar power is installed in Sweden?

As of march 2021 the accumlated installed power is above 1GW, in comparison, one of the three nuclear reactors at Forsmark, Sweden, has acapacity of approx. 1GW (source).

How much of the worlds electricity comes from solar?

In 2019 almost 3% of the electricity generation came from solar (source).
