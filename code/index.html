FAQ

What is a rain-mail?

A rain-mail is a personalized email with tips on how you can spend your perfect rainy saturday (or any other day) based on your answers in our form.

How often do you send out rain-mail?

Any time the forecast shows more than 50% chance of rain in your area, we create a collection of things you can do. Wether it's a nice brunch at a cafe or a new netflix show, we've got you covered. We even throw in some amazing cleaning hacks for those who loves to spend their rainy days tidying up at home.

Can i change my answers in the form?

Of course, just go to the form section on your profile and then click edit to change preferences.

Can i unsubscribe?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription anytime by just following the link down at the footer of the page.
