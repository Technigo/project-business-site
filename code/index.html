About Rainy Nights

Rainy Nights is an indipended publishing house based in New York. We sign publish both published and unpublished authors. As a house we mostly focus on works of fiction such as crime, romance and young adults novels but occationally we publish non fiction.

Rainy Nights is an indipended publishing house based in New York. We sign publish both published and unpublished authors. As a house we mostly focus on works of fiction such as crime, romance and young adults novels but occationally we publish non fiction.

Publish with us

To publish with us you need to fill out the form to establish contact. After that we will read you script/s and decide if it the right fit for us. If we want to publish the novel we will begin negotiations regarding contracts. If not we will give notes on the novel and hope you come back.

What happens next?

After signing the contract you are a part of Rainy nights Publishing. We will start with drafts and notes right away until we, together, feel that the novel is ready. The process of releasing a novel can be long and stressful but we will always be by your side.
