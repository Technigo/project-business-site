FAQ

Am I Too Old to Start Drumming?

Heck no! Drumming is the perfect exercise for the brain. It will keep you sharp!

I Don't Own a Drum Kit. Can I Still Learn?

It is difficult to practice without something to play on. Beginners can get away with only a practice pad. But soon you will need something a little more substantial in order to keep up you progess.

What If I Suck at Reading Notes?

The concern about music notes is that it will be too confusing. Learing to read notes is actually quite simple and is something a well rounded musican should be able to do. We give alot of exercises to students to learn the basic understanding of how ro read drum music. You will enjoy it!

Will weightlifting affect my drumming?

Weightlifting makes the muscles constrict and they need time to rebuild and relax. Being too tight can hamper your freedom of movement on the drums. Stretch after a work out and don't lift too heavily the day of a performance.
