FAQ

Are they good?

They're delicious, tasty and simply divine. They smell of cinnamon and are soft as a bun.

How long is the delivery time?

You have to order at least 2 days in advance. We let the dough cold-rise for at least 10 hours.

Can you make them gluten-free or vegan?

Of course! Just specify in the order form. For other allergies, send us an e-mail.

What if I want to order more than 99?

For bigger orders, send us an e-mail and we'll sort it out.

How many cinnamon rolls can I eat?

The recommended amount is at least 3.54 cinnamon rolls every day.
