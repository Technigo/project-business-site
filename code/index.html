FAQ

How big are the groups?

We arrange events for different types of groups, but no more than 10 people at the time.

What's the price?

It depends on your choices, we have differenct price plans for different needs. The explorations start at $1000 per person.

Do I need to bring anything?

Bring clothing and some hygiene products if you want to. We will provide food, drinks, equipment and so on.

What if I'm attacked by a wild animal?

If you're afraid of animals, this isn't for you. Maybe try a pottery class.
