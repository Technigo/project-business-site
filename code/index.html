Do I need to be a strong rider to participate?
Our cycling tours cater to all cycling abilities as well as non riding companions. We offer 2 riding distances each day and you can ride as much or as little as you wish. All our trips also include support vehicles that can give you a lift if needed. Our Non cycling tours are also very popular and are strictly non cycling.
When can I expect detailed daily schedule and rides itineraries?
Organizers of large events such as The Tour de France & the Giro will release the exact timing and detailed itinerary for each stage about 2 months in advance. Many of our daily activities are based upon the race timing. As information becomes available and our daily schedule is finalized, we will update you and your pre-departure packet will include all the details.
How do I meet my Tour guides at the Pick up location? What happens if I am late?
All our guided trips have 1 or more pick up location, date and time. Please see our Pick up/Drop off information for each tour. Your pre-travel information packet will also include an emergency phone number in the event that you are delayed and cannot make it to the pick up location in time. We will do our best to find a convenient solution and pick you up. However, if the delay is such that we cannot wait for you, you will have to make your own way to the first hotel.
