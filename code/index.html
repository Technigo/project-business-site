Frequently Asked Questions

Which time is the class?

The Masterclass is 10 am to 4 pm.

What about lunch?

There will be a 30 min lunch break and you should bring your own cold lunch.

What about coffee?

We have coffee! 😉 ☕

How much does it cost?

The Masterclass costs $85 and materials are included.

Should I bring my own apron?

You can borrow a clean apron from us. Please bring a scarf for your hair.

What happens if I can't make it to the class?

If you cancel at least 24 h before the class you will get your money back.

Can we eat what we bake?

Of course! You can bring your decorated cupcakes back home as well. We will provide a box.
