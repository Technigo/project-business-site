FAQ

Is the camp beginner friendly?
Definetly! Since more than half of our participants never stood up on a board before the camp we are very experienced in hosting total beginners. For surfing we split all camp participants into smaller groups based on previous experience and our coaches will take great care of you.
What is included?
Two daily surf lessons, one daily yoga class, shared accomondation (4 beds/room) and three deliciously nutritious vegetarian meals per day.
What equipment should bring with me?
We provide you with surfboards, and wetsuits if necessary. Other than that, you just need bring yourself, good spirits, and your choice of swimwear. For sun protection we recommend long sleeve rashguards.
