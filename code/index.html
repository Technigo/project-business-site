Time Frame

One window, four acts, one evening

A WINDOW IN TIME and A DOING EXHIBITION

Location

The exhibition takes places in a display window on Kungstensgatan 27, Stockholm. 'Kungstensgatan 27' is a nonprofit exhibition space showing site-specific installations in direct contact with the public streetscape. It is run by Eva Johansson, Einar Rodhe and Tim Gustafsson.

When

The exhibition will take place during the evening of May 7th, 17.00-20.00.

Artworks

'Helrenoverad' (2018), KONRAD LIDÉN
'Parser Building' (2019) PHILIP ULLMAN
'The Gaze' (2018), SALAD HILOWLE
A new performance by FATIMA MOALLIM
Interim ACT by IGOR BLOMBERG TRANÆUS

Welcome!

The exhibition will unravel during the evening as the artworks alternates in the window. 'Time Frame' deals with how to frame exhibition performativity and asks; what temporal forms are there to experience art?
