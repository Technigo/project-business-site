FAQ

How do we work?
After submitting your form, we give you a call and discuss the issue you and your dog are facing. Depending on the case we can decide for phone cosultations only or a home visit and assessment in real life, with 1:1 training, sometimes involving other dogs.
Do you work individually or in a group?
Our aim is to help as many as possible. Depending on dog's behavioural issue, we might combine several customers together, as it might speed up the recovery time.
