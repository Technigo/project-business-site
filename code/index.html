Tenko Museum of Modern Art

FAQS

If I want to visit the café, do I need to buy an entrance ticket?

Yes, you will need to buy an entrance ticket to visit the café.

Can I bring my own food?

If you bring food from outside the museum, you need to eat it in the park.

Is it allowed to take photos?

It is generally allowed to photograph without the use of flash.

Can I bring my dog?

Animals are not allowed in the park or in the galleries.
