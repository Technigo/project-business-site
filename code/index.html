Frequently Asked Questions

I am not green-fingered, which is the best plant for me?

There are many plants for Newbies.... the Zamioculcas Zamiiofolia however is the gold standard for Newbies. It tolerates neglect, low-light levels and drought very well.

Can you recommend a plant for a dark area?

Again the ZZ is great for darker areas, as are spider plants

How often should I water my plant?

It depends on the plant, but in General we are guilty of giving our plants too much love. Usually a good watering once-a-week is enough, although some plants benefit from a good misting on leaves and soil

My plant looks sad, how can I rescue it?

Usually the culprit is over-watering, so give the plant a few days to dry out and re-assess from there
