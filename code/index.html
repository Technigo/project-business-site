What is Alien Top Hat?
Alien Top Hat is a brand that designs Top Hats specifically for Aliens. We know it can be difficult finding a Top Hat that is designd to fit just your head perfectly and thats why we decided to create this brand. We make it possible for you to find the perfect Top Hat tailored to fit your alien head.
How do I know which color I should go with?
When it comes to which color you should choose for your Top Hat there are three simple tricks to remember:
  • Choose a color that complements your eyes.
  • If you have a green thumb your Top Hat color should be in a pink shade
  • Go with a color that makes you smile.
