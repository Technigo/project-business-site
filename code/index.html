FAQ

What is an Escape Room?
An escape room is a interactive game where you get 60 minutes to find a way out of the room by solving clues, codes and use the resources to break out into freedom.
How much does a Escape room cost?
The regular price is SEK 275 per person. Student and senior prizes (valid student card required, and at least 65 years respectively) are SEK 225 and children and youth prizes (up to 15 years) SEK 175 per person.
What are the payment options?
We take card payments and Swish. We do NOT accept cash. Companies that do not have the option of card payment are offered invoice payment.
What languages can you play in?
All rooms can be played in Swedish and English.
What happens if you do not break out in an hour?
You get released.
