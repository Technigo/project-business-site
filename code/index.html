FAQ

How will I know about planned events?

In addition to the weekly newsletter our volunteers are also getting a speacil invitations to all our extra activities, partneship events and mamy more. You are very welcome to fill up the form and sign in for newsletters.

Do I have to pay anything?

We believe that time is the strongest currency. Thus we are very happy to have such a wide network of people, who are not only sharing our ideas and belives, but also ready to dedicate thier time and passion to help us to ensure a brighter future for upcoming generations! So, as a volunteer, you are not supposed to pay anything extra and you are able to dedicate any amount of time towards our shared activities. Cya soon on one of out events!

How can I get my welcome pack?

Every our volunteer should receive a welcome gift from us within first two weeks of membership. If for any reason you didn't get yours, please don't hesitate to contact our Engagement manager via this email: members_support@gogreen.com. Our team will be happy to assist you!

Can I trust you to keep my personal details safe?

All processes related to members personal data are fully compliant with GDPR. You can withdraw you data from us at any point of time. In addition all our members are getting an annual update on thier membership status, personal data processing and general company activities.
