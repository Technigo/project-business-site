F.A.Q

How much does it cost?

It does not cost anything to be a member of the Read it Again community. And the book prices are decided by the seller.

What are the delivery options?

We can offer home delivery or to your nearest delivery point

How do I value my book?

You value the price based on the books condition. We have four categories Like new, Very good, Good and Acceptable. You can find more information about these in the section called Value your books.

How much is the shipping cost?

It is based on the weight and what delivery option you choose
