FAQ about Happy Petals

Where can I buy your flowers?

We have an online store where you can choose from different bouquets and delivery options or you can come visit us at our flower shop :)

What product types do you have?

We offer a wide selection of ready bouquet options for every situation, but we also have plenty of cut and dry flowers to create a result just right for you. In our flower store you can also find a selection of houseplants.

How can I book a workshop?

You can sign up for our workshops here on our webpage or you can contact us via email or phone.

What do I need for the workshop?

All the material used in the workshop is provided by us and is included in the workshop price. So just comfy clothes and an open mind is enough!

Do I need previous experience for the workshops?

All workshops are suitable for any level of experience from total beginners to more experienced crafters. For beginners we recommend especially the Flower Arrangements 101 workshop.
