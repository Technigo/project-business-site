FAQ

How much does it cost?➕

Adopting an echidna will cost you a monthly percentage of your total income, on top of an initial adminstrative fee calculated against your country of residence's GDP.

Are echidnas dog-friendly?➕

Your dog might be interested in your new echidna, but echidnas are disdainful to mingle with creatures so beneath their own dignity.

What do I feed my echidna?➕

Your echidna will expect to eat off your dinner plate.
