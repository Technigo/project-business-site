FAQ

What is this club about?

This is about enjoying the process of building fluency in Spanish while having fun and chatting with like-minded people.

Can I join if I don't speak Spanish at all?

Of course you can still join even if you don't speak a word of Spanish. However, we do reccomend that you fell kind of comfortable with a basic level in order to start, as it will help you take the maximum benefit of your time here.

How much does it cost?

It's free the first time you join us! After that, is only E19.99 per month, and you can join in as many times as you wish.

When can I start?

Anytime! Your starting date is completely up to you.
