FAQ

If you read some of the testimonies from people who've travelled to Afghanistan with us then you'll soon see what an amazing experience awaits. But you probably still have some questions. Here are our answers to some of the questions we get asked — frequently. Welcome to our FAQs...

Who are we?

Fethullah Tour is a Swedish-owned company and otur operator with an office in Stockholm. Founded in 1993 by founder Fethullah Dincer, we are the longest running tour company operating tours in Afghanistan

How much does a trip to Afghanistan cost?

Most introductory tours in Afghanistan will typically range between 600-1500 EUR per person depending on your point of departure, tour lenght, group size, accommodation type, rooming preference, and your method of transport.

Is it possible to visit Afghanistan?

Despite what the majority of people think, it's possible to visit North Korea as a tourist, and we have been doing so since 1993. The whole process is surprisingly easy — we can arrange it all for you, including travel, guides, food, accommodation, and even your Afghanistan visa.

What languages are the trips in?

All Fethullah Tours’ group trips are conducted in English. All tour leaders are native English speakers or highly proficient in English. For private independent tours, we often arrange local guides proficient in another language based upon request and availability. Please contact us for more details.

What about the food?

As a visitor and guest, you will be well fed with 3 meals a day including meat and fish. The Afghans take the role of host very seriously so they will always over-cater!

Do you offer any discounts?

Yes, students get 10% off on non-budget tours and there are also discounts for groups of people booking together. We unfortunately do not offer discounts for our budget tours.
