Do you play with them?

Yes! That's part of our job. We do what we love and we love what we do. We visit the cats 3 times a day, every 4 hours (to check on them, change their sand and fill up their food and water). We spend about 30 minutes playing with them or even more when we have time!

What's the longest period our cats can be there?

It happends that you have landed a dream job but that job is in another country. You may want to try out and be away for at least half a year. Send us an email and we will discuss this with you and figure out a solution together. The longest period that we a cat stayed with us so far is 6 months!

What brand of food and sand do you use?

We use a local Swedish brand! It's understandable if you want to bring what your cat is used to. 5% off if you bring your own food or sand. 10% if you bring both your own food and sand.
