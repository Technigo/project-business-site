I have questions about the project!

Period poverty?

It is the term used to describe a lack of access to menstrual hygiene products and education, toilets, hand washing facilities, and/or waste management. Even in developed countries, such as Sweden, period poverty exists and is a persistent problem!

Implications of period poverty?

  • Staying home from school and miss out on education
  • Using unhygienic and ineffective alternative methods (toilet paper, rags, etc.)
  • Experiencing shame, stress, and discomfort

Is it that expensive to have your period?

On average, menstruators endure 55 000 hours of their lives menstruating, use 10 000 hygiene products, and spend 63 000 SEK

What does it mean to be a period sponsor?

As a period sponsor you contributes to ensure that free menstrual hygiene products are handed out. By doing so, you make sure so that period poverty does not affect schooling or get in the way of physical and mental well-being.

You will also make a real impact, break the taboo, and create awareness to period poverty!
