The Details

About Our Classes

Our classes are held weeknights from 18-20, please check the calendar for details on specific courses. We also list information on teachers for each course directly in the calendar. All classes are limited to 12 people.

All of our teachers come highly recommended and have had formal culinary training. Many of them have food-trucks or eateries in town.

You do not need to bring anything but yourself. Aprons will be provided.

Pricing

Each class is 500:- which includes all cooking materials and any additional fixings for the group meal.

We will reach out for billing and to confirm your registration within two business days.

Location

Located at Köksgatan 23, we are in the heart of Stockholm.

T-Centralen is the closest metro station.

Please ring the bell to be let in.
