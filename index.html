Frequently Asked Questions

Who can take the course?

Anyone 18 years or older can take the course!

How many courses do you offer?

A beginners, intermediate, and advanced course.

What does the course include?

Espresso preparation, milk steaming, latte art training, equipment maintenance, coffee origin experience, and roasting fundamentals.

Can I pay in installments?

Yes, you can pay for the course in 3 installments.

Will I get a certicate after the course?

After completing the course you will receive an official barista certificate.
